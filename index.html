the features · and the truth

What it actually does.

We could lie to you like every other landing page. We won't. Here's the current state of things:

01 / watch
File watching
Watches your files. Tells you what changed. Sometimes tells you before you actually save. Time is a construct.
✓ works
02 / sync
Device sync
Sync between machines. In theory. In practice, it works if you're on the same wifi and Mercury isn't in retrograde.
~ mostly
03 / recall
Command history
Search every command you've ever typed. Including that rm -rf you regret. We remember. Forever.
✓ works
04 / share
Session sharing
Share your terminal with a friend. Neither of you will remember why after 5 minutes. This feature is buggy.
✗ broken
05 / soon
AI, obviously
Every dev tool has AI now. So do we. Kind of. It suggests you take breaks. That's the whole feature.
